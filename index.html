Olivier Lévêque

I'm an Early-stage Researcher at Charles Fabry Laboratory working on Optimization and Image Processing for Optics. I've received my master diploma in Electrical Engineering in 2017 from the École normale supérieure Paris-Saclay, followed by a second master in Automatic Control and Signal Processing in 2018 from the Université Paris-Saclay. I'm passionate by Robotics. During my undergraduate studies, I've cofounded Mr. Robotics, an open source software and hardware project to easily build an autonomous robot. Hopefully, I'll be back soon to this project, but for the moment, there is a PhD going on!
